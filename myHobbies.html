THE FIRST TIME I WAS EXPOSED TO TAKING PHOTOS WITH A DIGITAL CAMERA WAS BACK IN THE SPRING OF MY FRESHMAN YEAR. MY STEPDAD HAD GOT MY MOM A CAMERA FOR A VACATION TRIP TO HAWAII TO TAKE FAMILY PHOTOS. THOUGH THE CAMERA'S PURPOSE WAS FOR "FAMILY PHOTOS", WHENEVER THE CAMERA WASN'T BEING USED TO TAKE I WAS GOING AROUND SNAPPING PICTURES OF WILDLFIE, BUILDINGS,A ND PEOPLE. AS FOR TODAY, I TAKE WHENEVER I FEEL THE URGE TO TAKE PHOTOS I JUST GRAB MY CAMERA AND GO OUT A SHOOT."

C H E S S








"I BELIEVE THAT CHESS IS ONE THE BEST STRATIGICAL GAMES IN THE WORLD. WHEN I WAS A KID, MY DAD SAT ME DOWN A TAUGHT ME THE RULES OF CHESS. THIS MENTALLY DEMANDING GAME LATER BECAME THE "THE FATHER SON GAME" THAT SYMBOLIZED OUR RELATIONSHIP. HOWEVER, AFTER THE SEPARATION OF MY PARENTS I STOPPED PLAYING WITH MY DAD AND ONLY PLAYED OCCASIONALLY WITH FRIENDS. HOWEVER RECENTLY, ONE OF MY FRIENDS LANCE (BIG CHESS GUY) ACTUALLY GOT ME BACK INTO PLAYING IT. I AM IN NO WAY BETTER THAN HIM, BUT I ENJOY PLAYING CHESS WITH HIM."

[LEFT: A PHOTO I TOOK OF MY GLASS CHESSBOARD BACK HOME]

"I OCCASIONALLY PLAY MY SAXOPHONE. BACK IN MIDDLE SCHOOL I DECIDED TO JOIN MY SCHOOL'S BAND AND THE INSTRUMENT I CHOSE TO PLAY WAS THE ALTO SAXOPHONE. I PLAYED ALL THROUGH MIDDLE AND HIGH SCHOOL. PATRICK'S DAY PARADE IN IRELAND, PLAY FOR A PROSCHE EVENT ANNOUNING THE 2019 PORSCHE CARRERA, PERFORMING AT DISNEYLAND, AND MUCH MORE. TODAY, I RARELY TAKE MY SAXOHONE OUT TO PLAY BUT WHEN I DO I HAVE A GOOD TIME.
